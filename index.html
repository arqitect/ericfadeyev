About Me

I am a sophomore at the University of California, Santa Cruz, pursuing a B.S. in Bioengineering with a concentration in Bioinformatics. My academic journey is driven by a fascination with the potential of computational tools to solve complex biological problems. Alongside my core studies, I am also diving deep into Computer Science to build a robust technical foundation.

Beyond the lab and the command line, I am passionate about visual storytelling through photography and filmography. These creative outlets allow me to capture moments, explore different perspectives, and communicate ideas in a powerful way. I am always looking for opportunities to merge my technical skills with my creative interests.
